The Best Movies according to Prisha

My top three movies of all time

Zindagi Na Milegi Dubara

A fantastic movie about three friends, who go on a life changing trip. You learn to respect your life and live it instead just survive

Ye Jawaani Hai Deewani

Again a movie about friendship, love, life and how they all change over time but the essence remains the same. The movie potrays the importance of relationships.

Dil Chahta Hai

Also, a movie about three friends finding love with different perspective, ideologies. This movie breaks superstitions and prejudices with its remarkable cast and screenplay
